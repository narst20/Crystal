Welcome to the Suit Game!

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four playing card suits below. By clicking on a card suit, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random nuber.

Be careful! The value of each card suit is hidden from you until you click it.

Each time when the game starts over, the game will change the values of each card suit.



Good luck!
